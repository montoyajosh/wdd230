Target Audience

Idaho Weather Solutions Target Audience

The target audience of Idaho Weather Solutions are:

  • Who: Students, Office workers, Farmers, and the residents inside Idaho
  • Age: 18 to 80
  • Motivation: To plan their activities and work.
